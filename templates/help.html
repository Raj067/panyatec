Roles of administrator

    Opening the account of the school(Administrator account).
    Adding new user to the system including teachers, secretaries and academics.
    Authorize the process of preview and printing of the results and other analysis files associated with the results.
    Setting the profile of the school.
    Regulation of the user by activating, deactivating and deleting them.
    Can access users information such as their profiles
    Can views all the activities going in his or her organization account.
    Make payments
    Send notifications to teachers
 
 

Roles of academic

    Preparing the list of the subjects by adding them to the database.
    Adding students to the database or deleting them.
    Send forms generated by using the students' names to teacher so as to be filled
    Deciding the uses of numbers or the uses of names during the process of sending the students' list form to teachers.
    Can add or delete subject from the database
    Can send notifications to other teachers.
    Authorize teachers when to start filling the form and the deadline of submission.
    Receive the marks form from teachers and joining them so as to generate table of results
    Can regulate the results by any means he/she wants.
    Can preview the results, and send the previewed results to administration for authorization
 
 

Roles of teacher

    Filling the students' marks forms and submitting them to academic.
    For subjects with practical and theory papers, teacher can select the appropriate formula for his subject.
    Receive notifications from administrator and academics
 
 

Roles of secretary

    Downloading and printing the results after being authorized by administrator.
